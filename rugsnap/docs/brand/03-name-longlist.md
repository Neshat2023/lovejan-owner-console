- **Famous-mark collisions** (25 Hallmark, 35 Pedigree, 66 Kanon/Canon, 78 Zarra/Zara): a name that requires litigating a giant is not a name.
- **Existing same-space brands** (29 Origyn — authentication space itself): direct-category conflicts are disqualifying even pre-counsel.
- **Dictionary generics without modification** (24 Assay, 45 Discern, 61 Holdings): unprotectable alone; usable only in compounds.
- **Slang/sensitivity risks** (78 Zayin): multilingual screening kills early.
- **"Snap" family names** (ArtSnap, CoinSnap, WatchSnap — deliberately absent): architecture doc 02 rejected the family strategy; adding more Snap marks multiplies UNVERIFIED risk adjacent to Snap Inc.
