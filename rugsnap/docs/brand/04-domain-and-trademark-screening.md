## Search/SEO & discoverability (finalists)

- **Veluma:** near-zero search noise (coined) → owns page one quickly; voice-search safe; misspelling risk low ("valuma" defensive worth registering). Brand-SEO play, zero category-keyword equity — which is correct: category SEO belongs to product pages, not the company name.
- **Vitrine:** heavy generic noise (furniture/display-case results) in FR/PT markets; EN noise moderate; would take years to own the word globally. FA search: strong familiarity, low competition.
- **Valyt:** zero noise; but .ai-primary brands leak type-in traffic to the .com owner — accept only with eventual .com acquisition intent.
- **Appra:** noise from appraisal-tool startups (signal of crowding); voice-search collides with "opera" in some accents.
- **Trove-family:** noisy; multiple defunct/active Troves confuse branded search.
- General rule recorded: exact-match keyword domains are NOT the goal — brand uniqueness + product-page keyword strategy beats keyword domains for a trust brand.
