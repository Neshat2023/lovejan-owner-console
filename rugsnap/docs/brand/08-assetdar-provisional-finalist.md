## Pre-approval checklist (required before any final approval)
Status key: ✅ done (from this environment, non-authoritative unless noted) · ⚠️ finding to weigh · ⛔ requires qualified counsel.

1. **Exact & similar trademark search** — ⛔ *requires counsel.* Search-level (non-authoritative), 2026-07-23: no exact “Assetdar” word mark surfaced. Similar/adjacent noise is dominated by crypto “DAR” entities (see item 5). A formal USPTO/WIPO/EUIPO clearance by an attorney is still mandatory.
2. **Common-law company & product search** — ✅ search-level, 2026-07-23: no exact “Assetdar” company/product found. Nearest users are acronym-“DAR” crypto/finance entities, not the word “Assetdar.”
3. **Classes 9, 35, 36, 42 review** — ⛔ *requires counsel* (downloadable software 9; retail/marketplace 35; financial/valuation 36; SaaS/AI 42). Class 36 is the sensitivity zone given the crypto-“DAR” overlap.
4. **English radio & spelling test** — ⚠️ said aloud, “ASSET-dar” is reasonably clear, but spelling from hearing is ambiguous (assetdar / assetdare / assetdarr / aset-dar), and a finance-aware listener may parse it as “Asset D-A-R” (letters) rather than a word — a mild but real friction. Recommend a live test with non-Persian English speakers before approval.
5. **Financial/crypto confusion testing** — ⚠️ **material finding.** “DAR” is an active crypto ticker — **Digital Asset Rights Token (DAR)** (Ethereum, listed on Crypto.com/Bitget/CoinMarketCap/Yahoo Finance) — plus **Digital Asset Research (DAR)** (institutional crypto data), **DARA (Digital Asset Regulatory Authority)**, and DART’s/DARTS (NFT chain). To a global/finance/crypto-aware audience, “Asset-DAR” can read as a **digital-asset / tokenization** product. For a brand whose entire positioning is *physical* valuables + anti-fraud + trust, being mistaken for a crypto token is a genuine risk. **This is the strongest single caution against Assetdar and must be weighed before final approval.**
6. **Final live domain & renewal-price verification** — ✅ 2026-07-23: `assetdar.com` **available at standard, non-premium registration $11.25 USD / 1 yr** (Vercel registrar quote; not flagged premium). Renewal is standard `.com` (confirm exact renewal figure at the founder’s checkout screen before reserving).
