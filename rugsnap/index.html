The appraisal

What one photo reveals

The same signals a dealer reads in the bazaar — structure, style, age, and honesty about condition.

Weave & knots

Hand-knotted or machine-made, Turkish or Persian knot, and an estimate of density per square inch.

Region & school

Tabriz or Kashan, Qajar oil or nastaliq panel — the tradition your piece belongs to, and why.

Era & materials

Approximate age, wool or silk, oil or ink, and whether dyes and pigments read natural or synthetic.

An honest value

A conservative USD range with a stated confidence level — never oversold, never underspoken.
