What one photo reveals

Weave & knots

Hand-knotted or machine-made, symmetric Turkish or asymmetric Persian knot, and an estimate of density per square inch.

Region & tradition

Tabriz or Kashan, Heriz or Nain, Qashqai or Baluch — the workshop tradition your piece belongs to, and why.

Era & materials

Approximate age, wool or silk pile, cotton or wool foundation, and whether the dyes read as natural or synthetic.

An honest value

A conservative USD market range with a stated confidence level — so a family treasure never leaves for less than it should.
